Year-end Review of 2024

My Favorite Watches of this Year

1. Grave of the Fireflies

This hauntingly beautiful animated film presents a heart-wrenching tale of survival, loss, and a must-watch for everyone as it explains the severity and the gruesome reality of war through first-person pov. This cinematic masterpiece undoubtedly stands as one of the finest wartime films ever created. It can make you truly appreciate the good things in life and remind you how much your family and life itself mean to you.

2. Arcane : League of Legends

Arcane is not just a show; it's an emotional odyssey, an experience that transcends its medium with outstanding visuals. A Masterpiece That Redefines Animated Storytelling. Each character's journey is intricately crafted, and their struggles feel intensely real, raw, and human. Never before has a series made me feel such a kaleidoscope of emotions, from elation to heartbreak, from awe to deep introspection.

3. Saripodhaa Sanivaram

A man with anger issues vents his frustration only on Saturdays, as promised to his late mother, to keep his temper in check. The rest of the story revolves around how he finds a purpose for his anger and fights for the right reasons. The story may sound funny or silly at first glance, but the way the screenplay is written makes every minute worth your time. The characters are well-drawn, making you either root for them or hate them.

4. Mathu Vadalara 2

The screenplay and the storyline of this movie was excellent which kept the audience on the edge of their seats, all the while keeping them entertained with the character's humor, dialogue and impeccable acting. The comedy of this film definitely stole the show. The sequel has done justice to the expectations the first movie of this film series set, if not exceeded them.

5. Laapatha Ladies

I have to say, Laapataa Ladies is a film that truly stands out. From the moment I started watching, I was captivated by its charm and depth. The storytelling is nothing short of brilliant. Each character is meticulously developed, and their stories intertwine seamlessly, creating a rich tapestry of human emotions and relationships. The cast delivers exceptional performances, bringing authenticity and heart to their roles.
